High School Choir

The High School Choir is the only level of vocal performing ensemble at Forest Area. High School choir students will learn refinement and mastery of fundamentals and musicality from the Scott Rush book Habits of a Successful Choral Musician.

Performances

The High School Choir performs at the Winter Concert, District Festival and the spring musical. The choir also performs a Veterans Day ceremony at the school.
