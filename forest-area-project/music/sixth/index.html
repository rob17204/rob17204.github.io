6th Grade Band

6th Grade band is the first level of performing ensemble at Forest Area.
It is instrumental based and students will learn the theory of music, and basic fundamentals of musicality.
The book we use is Premier Performance by Ed Sueta.

Performances:

The 6th Grade band will have two performances -
the Winter and the Spring Concert.
6th Grade band students will have the opportunity if they choose to help with High School Band and Choir performances such as parades and festivals.
