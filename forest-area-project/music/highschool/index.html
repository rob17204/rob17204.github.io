High School Band

The High School Band is the highest level of instrumental performing ensemble at Forest Area. High School band students will learn refinement and mastery of fundamentals and musicality from the Scott Rush book Habits of a Successful Musician.

Performances

The High School Band performs in both concert and marching venues. Marching performances include home football games, the Cadillac Marching Review and the District Marching Expo at Thirlby Field. In spring the marching band performs at the Trout Festival parade in Kalkaska and the 4th of July parade in Fife Lake. For concert band, the High School Band performs at the Winter and Spring Concerts and District Festival, as well as random events in Fife Lake as requested by the community.
